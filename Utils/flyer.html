Event Details

Round-1: CARDS GAMBIT

 Participants will be given two sets of cards, each containing 13 cards. On picking every card, a question will appear. Here these questions are based on aptitude, technology, general knowledge, etc. There is a timer for the entire round. Whoever answers the maximum number of questions within the stated time limit will qualify for the next round.
 

Round-2: Invenire Objecto

Participants will be provided with an AI object detection app. After successfully logging in, a riddle will be displayed to every player. The participant will have to solve the riddle to find the object and they will then be sent on a hunt to scan the object. Most of these objects can be found in the college. The top participants who scan the objects correctly will qualify for the third round.
 

Round-3: AMMISIO

Mock interviews simulate a real conversation between an HR, TR and MR for the desired position. Participants will not only train the skill of participating in interviews, but also receive feedback and advice on what needs to be improved. The panel will decide the best team according to their evaluation criteria.
